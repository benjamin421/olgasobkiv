Olga Sobkiv

Оля собків

Olga Sobkiv is a skilled maker and an aspiring architect based in Chicago, IL with interests in structural tectonics, fabrication, and studio art. Growing up between two vastly different cultures, the United States and Ukraine, guides Olga’s desire to explore architectural design in an international context. After earning a Bachelor of Science in Architecture and a minor in Studio Art from Washington University in St. Louis in 2021, Olga will pursue her Masters in Architecture at the Bartlett School of Architecture in London, graduating in 2025. Olga is currently taking a year off from her studies to work on an independent project. Her portfolio showcases her mastery of architecture, furniture design, and fine art.

Design as Play

A design philosophy that capitalizes on the importance of play. Creating spaces and objects that are a celebration of the richly eccentric and unusual.

Design through Making

Making is valued as a design process. An emphasis on physical trial and error, fosters opportunities for novel design discoveries.

Design with Tectonics

Seeking novel methods of expressing structural tectonics within architecture and design. Inspired by Kenneth Frampton's book "Studies in Tectonic Culture".
